Bio

I am a final-year Computer Science and Technology student at Tsinghua University. I have an academic foundation built on coursework in areas such as Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Data Mining, Web Development (Frontend), Operating Systems, and Data Structures.

In my most recent internship, I worked as a Quantitative Alpha Researcher at Trexquant Investment LP, where I focused on developing event-driven trading signals, or alphas, and built a machine learning model to optimize the combination of these alphas for trading strategies, particularly for earnings announcement prediction.

I am currently working on my final project for Deep Learning Course, where I am developing a Sign Language (ASL) to Text Translation using a CNN-RNN architecture.

I am from Bangkok, Thailand, but am currently based in Beijing, China.
